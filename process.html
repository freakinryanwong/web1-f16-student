CLIENT:

LEXI DELEON ILLUSTRATIONS

A renowned painter and portrait artist, Lexi DeLeon is a 21-years-old illustrator. Her astounding work is mostly created with her fundamental form of media, gouache. Drafting in inks dribbles or graphite scribbles, her work can span across many different aesthetic choices before her final form of gouache. Her work is notable by the linework that fall into a mess of beautiful flowy and streaming lines. She will need a well-developed portfolio website to showcase in the future. The look for her website would have to be minimalistic, simple and very user friendly, with an easy navigation system and a friendly use for the blogging system. Her objective is to be able to exhibit her artwork in a simple, organized manner, while keeping the ornate decorative aesthetic that aligns well with her paintings.
